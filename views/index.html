About

Gain Confidence With a Click of a Button

Analyze your email text right form your gmail account with a push of a button. Tonalyze uses linguistic analysis to detect three types of tones from your email text: emotions, social tendencies, and writing style, all at the convenience of your personal button. Analyze text as mush as you need to get the result you are looking for. Use the results to determine if your writing comes across the way you intend for it to and send your email with confidence.

In the early-1800's, peppered moths lived on lichen-covered birch trees and were white with black speckles, making them blend in with their environment. An all-black variant of the peppered moth was virtually unknown.

After the industrial revolution, soot was pumped out into the atmosphere, changing the moths' habitat by making the trees dark and bare. Moths that were previously well camouflaged became easy prey, and the black variant of the moth began to thrive. Within a century, most of the moths in the region were black.

As environmental regulations became more strict and the air was cleaned up, the trees became light again—and the moth population once again changed. Within decades, white peppered moths were abundant, and black ones were rare.

The Tone Analyzer Service helps people to understand three categories of tone scores: emotional tone, social tone, and language tone. Each category has several sub-tones that the service scores. These tables describe each emotion or category, and how to interpret the score the service returns for that emotion.

Currently, only English text can be analyzed.

Sentences with less than three words do not return a response.
